Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to complete a website?

The timeline depends on the complexity of the project. A simple business website typically takes 2-4 weeks, while a complex e-commerce platform may take 6-8 weeks or more. We'll provide a specific timeline after our initial consultation.

Do you provide ongoing support after the website is launched?

Yes, I offer various maintenance and support packages to ensure your website continues to function optimally after launch. This includes updates, security checks, backups, and technical support.

Can you work with existing websites or do you only build from scratch?

I can do both! Whether you need a brand new website built from scratch or want to redesign/improve an existing website, I can help. We can discuss the best approach during our consultation.

What payment methods do you accept?

I accept various payment methods including bank transfers, mobile money, and online payment platforms. Typically, a 50% deposit is required to begin work, with the remaining 50% due upon completion.

Do you offer discounts for non-profit organizations or startups?

Yes, I offer special pricing for non-profit organizations and early-stage startups. Please mention your organization type when submitting your order, and we'll discuss applicable discounts during our consultation.
